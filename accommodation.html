RENTAL INFORMATION

So now that you are done with the applications and have your visa stamped on your passport,the next step is finding a place to live. Canada offers both On-Campus and Off-Campus housing options. On-campus housing is convenient but may have limited availability and higher costs. Off-campus housing provides more independence but requires managing utilities and commute.

Searching for accommodation in Canada can be exciting and challenging. Consider location, the rental market, and utilize online platforms for a successful search. Choose the option that aligns with your preferences, budget, and lifestyle, and start your search early to secure suitable accommodation.


IMPORTANT TIPS

Here are some tips for finding a rental property in Canada as an incoming international student:

  • Start Early
  • Use Online Rental Platforms
  • Check University Resources
  • Set a Budget
  • Explore Shared Housing
  • Beware of Scams
  • Request Virtual Tours
  • Connect with Student Communities
  • Negotiate Wisely

Finally, and perhaps most importantly, STAY PATIENT
