ABOUT

We are a group of recent grads, previously international students now advising new international students coming to Canada. Our objective is helping newcomers navigate their educational journey and adjust to life in a new country.

Since we struggled ourselves with issues you might encounter we may be able to offer some aid and tips.

Our firsthand experience and knowledge could provide valuable insights and support to make your transition smoother.
