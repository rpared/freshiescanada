ABOUT US

Welcome to our website, your comprehensive guide to transportation, accommodation, and part-time job opportunities for international students in Toronto. We understand the challenges faced by students in a new city, and we are here to provide you with the information you need to navigate your way through this exciting journey. At our website, we strive to empower international students by equipping them with the knowledge and resources they need to thrive in Toronto. We aim to be your go-to source for all things related to transportation, accommodation, and part-time jobs. Our team is dedicated to providing accurate and up-to-date information, ensuring you have a smooth and successful experience during your time in Toronto.

Team Alpha comprises Roger, Amandeep and Karina. We have teamed up to build a website which would be convient for the freshers. Be sure to take a look at our services. We are a group of recent grads, previously international students now advising new international students coming to Canada. Our objective is helping newcomers navigate their educational journey and adjust to life in a new country. Since we struggled ourselves with issues you might encounter we may be able to offer some aid and tips. Our firsthand experience and knowledge could provide valuable insights and support to make your transition smoother. Whether you are a new student or have been in Toronto for a while, we are here to support you every step of the way. Explore our website and let us be your trusted companion in making your study abroad journey in Toronto unforgettable.


SERVICES

Freshies in Canada is an information website specifically designed to assist new international students in their journey of studying and living in Canada. Our comprehensive services cover a wide range of areas, including documentation, accommodation, SIM cards, transportation, part-time jobs, recreation, and money-saving tips. We provide valuable resources, guidance, and practical advice to ensure a smooth and successful transition for international students.

Whether you need assistance with visa documentation, finding suitable accommodation, obtaining a SIM card, navigating transportation options, securing part-time employment, discovering recreational activities, or managing your finances, Freshies in Canada is here to support you every step of the way. Our goal is to empower and equip you with the knowledge and resources needed to make the most of your experience as an international student in Canada. Join us at Freshies in Canada and embark on an enriching adventure, Where your dream of studying in Canada starts here, and we're excited to be a part of your extraordinary journey. Welcome to Freshies in Canada!
