DOCUMENTATION

FOR APPLICATION

You need these documents to apply for a study permit:

  • proof of acceptance
  • proof of identity
  • proof of financial support

You may also need a letter of explanation an attestation of issuance of your Quebec Acceptance Certificate (CAQ) a medical exam a custodian declaration (minors only), or other documents.

Once you enter Canada you will receive the Study Permit and a SIN number. Be sure to check them throughoutly in the airport, and ask any questions regarding them.
